The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearence to the much larger foxhound the beagle was developed primarily in hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuff in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
